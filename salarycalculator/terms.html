2. Cookie Policy

UK PECR Compliance: We use cookies to improve your experience and analyze site usage. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience and understand how our site is used.

2.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

  • Session Cookies: Remember your calculator inputs during your visit
  • Security Cookies: Protect against malicious activity
  • Legal Basis: Necessary for site functionality (no consent required)

Analytics Cookies (Require Consent)

  • Google Analytics: _ga, _ga_*, _gid
  • Purpose: Understand site usage, popular calculators, user behavior
  • Duration: 2 years (GA cookies)
  • Data Shared: Google Ireland Limited

Advertising Cookies (If AdSense Used)

  • Google AdSense: Various advertising cookies
  • Purpose: Show relevant advertisements
  • Data Shared: Google Ireland Limited

2.3 Managing Cookies

Cookie Banner Consent

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
